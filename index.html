What is AST

A prestigious test, built to be fair.

The AcademeForge Scholars Test (AST) identifies and rewards talented students from Classes 1 to 10. Conducted offline in registered schools and coaching centers, it assesses understanding, critical thinking, and problem-solving across subjects — giving every student a genuine platform to challenge themselves and be recognised on merit.

Regional & school rankings

Every student receives a clear rank, both within their own school and across the wider region.

Real exam-like practice

A printed paper, a strict hour, and a marking scheme that rewards accuracy — exactly like the exams that matter later.

Detailed performance feedback

Beyond a score — students learn exactly where their strengths and gaps are, subject by subject.

Eligibility

Open to every student, Class 1 through 10.

Students register through their respective school or coaching center, not individually off the street — this keeps the test grounded in real classrooms and easy to administer offline. The paper is built to flex with ability, mixing basic and advanced questions so it's fair whether a student is just starting out or already exam-sharp.

  • 01 Open to Classes 1–10, no prior test required to enter.
  • 02 Registration is routed through the student's school or coaching center.
  • 03 Question difficulty blends basic and advanced, by class.

Rules & evaluation

Fair for everyone, every time.

  • ! Students must follow all examination guidelines issued by their school or center.
  • ! Any cheating or malpractice leads to immediate disqualification.
  • ! Results declared by the AST panel are final and cannot be contested.
